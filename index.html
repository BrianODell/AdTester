About Mutt Strut

It’s The Greatest Spectacle in Dog Walking!

Mutt Strut is a one-of a-kind event for pets of all shapes and sizes! Join the 7,000 people and 6,000 pets who attend Mutt Strut each year from across Indiana! Walk the historic Indianapolis Motor Speedway track and kiss the bricks, enjoy a fun day outdoors with family and furry friends, and support the Humane Society of Indianapolis. This year, we hope you’ll join us and consider registering your own Mutt Strut Pack (team).

Why has Mutt Strut been voted Best Charitable Event in Indianapolis by NUVO readers the last eight years in a row? Join us – you’ll see!

Date: Saturday, April 29, 2017.

Time: 9:30 am to 3 pm. Registration opens at 9:30 am. The official Mutt Strut walk kicks off at 11 am! Music, vendors, food, and activities are available from 9:30 am to 3 pm.

Place: The Indianapolis Motor Speedway. You can see a map and get directions here.

Mutt Strut at the Indianapolis Motor Speedway offers the Party on the Plaza with vendors and food, a photo opportunity at the Yard of Bricks, a walk along the Grand Prix track, demonstrations by the Indy Dog & Disc Club, and more!
